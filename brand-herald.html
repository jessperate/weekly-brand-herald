Campaign of the Quarter
Feb 18  ·  The Marketype Quiz

Seven archetypes. One quiz. The whole company in the trenches.

Pat Szot, Cyris Talban, Kate Marshall, Jordan Miller, Travis Tyler, Jess Rosenberg, Asad Ullah

The Marketype Quiz was the brand team's most ambitious project yet: an interactive archetype experience with seven player types (Vision, Glue, Maverick, Spark, Craft, Flex, Heart), shareable player cards designed for LinkedIn virality, an Austin airport billboard, custom Lottie loading animations, and a quiz burndown that had the team heads-down from Feb 11 to launch day.

Pat and Cyris delivered the design system. Kate locked every line of copy. Jordan and Trav drove distribution strategy. Jess built the live experience in code -- testing the form under traffic load from a plane on the tarmac.

"Happy Marketype Day! The most beautiful one pager I have ever seen."

-- Travis Tyler, Feb 20, writing from the AEO Conference floor

The AEO Conference the next day was the proof of concept. Jess reported back: "You guys are everywhere right now." The Graphite team gushed over the brand details. The rugby shirts were a hit. Kate Marshall signed off on her last contract day that same week, leaving a note: "There's so much excellence here."
